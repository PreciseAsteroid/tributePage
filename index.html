Boaz Neumann

1970-1915

It is not me, it is him... Refering to Hitler in an explenation to why his courses are in high demand

Back in uni we were allowed to pick two courses from any discpline we wanted. Being a history beef, with a particular interest in Nazi Germani, picking the right course was easy. Boaz was the lecturer of the course named Introduction to Nazism I ended up picking. I was so impressed with his fresh look and deep knowledge that I continued following him from afar. Last year I learned that he died after suffering from the cancer desease. Even though I did not know him at all I felt very sad. I guess we feel like that when we believe the world had lost a unique mind.

Time Line

In Points

  • You can learn nothing from Histroy. Every place in time has its own unique language and voice which are irrelevant for other events
  • My position is non judgemental. I try to understand and to accept the values and terms and above all the language of the era
  • History classes should develop original thinkers and not focus on memorizing facts
